# The frozen Gate-0 L0 grammar

> **Status:** `reference` — foundational design artifact (2026-07-04). **NOT SOURCE OF TRUTH** — a
> design contract; shipped source + the frozen upstream contracts win (Q-0120).

> **What this is.** This file is the single, ratified **manifest-grammar spec** for the SuperBot
> rebuild's **L0 kernel-spine + cross-cutting grammar** — the consolidation of every pinned
> field / enum / port / table / fence / facet the 14 foundational-design specs freeze, folded into
> **one manifest a builder reads top-to-bottom** to author the `sb/spec/` dataclasses (from **this**
> file, not by re-reading 14 specs). It extends `rebuild-design-spec-2026-07-02.md` §2/§3 with the
> 87 primitives harvested in the Gate-0 grammar-freeze fold. Every field is cross-referenced to its
> **owning spec §** — SOURCE WINS (Q-0120): where a slice found the work-list mis-cited a shape, the
> row was re-verified against the owning spec §3 and the correction is recorded inline (`MISCITE:`)
> and consolidated in the closing "Mis-cites corrected against source" subsection. Role tags:
> **[S]** hand-authored (sim-frozen) · **[A]** arrangement (sim overlay) · **[O]** objective /
> telemetry · **[DERIVED]** compiler-computed, not authored · **(primitive)** = a
> type/enum/port/table/fence/facet/marker, not a field on an existing spec.
>
> **NOTE — owner-only defaults are owner-pending.** A small number of *default* values in this
> grammar are **owner-gated and NOT ratified by this freeze** — the *shape* is frozen, the shipped
> *default* is proposed and awaits the owner-decision packet
> (`gate-0/owner-decision-packet.md`). The load-bearing one is the **Group 17 intent
> `posture=DEGRADE` / `required=False` flip** (owner-gate **PG-2 / Q-D5**, seam-matrix open-fork
> **F-3**): the frozen shared-vocabulary **floor keeps `required=True` fail-closed** until the owner
> rules; DEGRADE is the design *recommendation*, not the shipped floor. Other labeled owner-gated
> deferrals: `version_policy` default for a `bears_value` store (§8.1 OD-1, Group 11), `SB_PROD_ATTEST`
> custody mechanism (Group 5/14), owner-override scope + transparency-sink wording (§8, Group 4),
> and the `cutover_flip_ts` home + `trigger_epoch` derivation (Groups 12/13). Each is flagged at its
> row; none blocks the freeze.
>
> **NOTE — the four pending cross-spec wirings.** Four shapes in this grammar are *consumed here* but
> **land on their target spec as a Gate-0 wiring closure**, not yet applied in the strand specs:
> `ActorRef.member_tier` (RC-12, Group 2), the spec-02 absorption of 04's authority contracts
> (RC-2/3/4/5/13/14/15, Group 4), `WorkflowContext.test_mode` (Group 8), and the `ChannelEmitter`
> egress-port registration on 02/K8 (RC-21, Group 16). Each is specified as a **precise, ready-to-apply
> edit** in the sibling doc **`gate-0/cross-spec-wiring.md`** (Closures 1–4, `CLOSED-AT-GATE-0`) — the
> builder applies those edits when authoring the corresponding `sb/spec/` / `sb/kernel/` module.
